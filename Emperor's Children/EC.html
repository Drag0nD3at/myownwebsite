Emperor's Children

About

The Emperor's Children are a Former Space Marine Chapter that now belongs in the clutches of the Chaos God Slanesh. During the Horus Heresy, The Primarch, Fulgrim, allied himself with his brother Horus in the fight against the Emperor.

Emperor's Children

Emperor's Children are led by the Primarch Fulgrim, known for his charisma and ego. Think of a Pretty Boy who is extremely jealous and what's the best of everything and you got Fulgrim.
