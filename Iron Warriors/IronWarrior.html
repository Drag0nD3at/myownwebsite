Iron Warriors

About the Iron Warriors

The Iron Warriors are known for siege warfare and the reduction of fortified positions. They were one of the 9 legions to fall to chaos. During the Horus Heresy, The Iron Warriors often fought with the Imperial Fists, as they were experts on defensive tactics and fortifications.
